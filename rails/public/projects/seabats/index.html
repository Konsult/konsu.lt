There is Magic in the Land

Once you visit South East Asia, your life will never be the same. There's a magic in Asia, be it the Buddhist Monks that bring an amazing spirituality to the region, maybe it's the kindest people in the world, maybe it's the golden temples in the middle of nowhere, or maybe it's the food. South East Asia is such an amazing place, and it will probably be the most amazing experience of your life.

Experience a full Cultural Immersion

Why do people get stressed on vacations? Because they try to cram too much into too little time, and end up only remembering everything as a blur. Vacations are about slowing down and and smelling the papaya. When you have time to savor each experience, you'll be able to remember and learn from so much of your experience. Many people will say they can't afford to leave their normal lives for 30 days… but we say, how can you afford to forgo one of the most magical, life-changing experiences you will ever have?

“Travel is more than the seeing of sites; it is a change that goes on, deep and permanent, in the ideas of living”
